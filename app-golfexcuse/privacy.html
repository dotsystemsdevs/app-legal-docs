Privacy Policy

Golf Excuse Generator
Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Golf Excuse Generator respects your privacy and is designed to work without collecting personal data.

1. Data Collection

Golf Excuse Generator does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

Any local preferences (such as whether you have been asked to rate the app) are stored only on your device and are never transmitted.

2. No Servers or Third Parties

The app does not use any servers, cloud services, or third-party analytics or advertising networks for tracking. Excuses are chosen at random on your device.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Since no personal data is collected or processed, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) does not apply beyond local storage on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children. No personal information from users of any age is stored or processed.
