# Privacy Policy

**Spoke - AI Bike Diagnostic Tool**

*Last updated: January 2026*

## Overview

Spoke ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Recordings
- We temporarily process audio recordings of your bicycle sounds
- Audio is sent to our secure servers for AI analysis
- Recordings are **not stored permanently** and are deleted after processing

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Anonymous usage analytics

### Data You Provide
- API key (stored securely on your device)
- Feedback on diagnosis accuracy (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Audio Analysis**: To diagnose bicycle issues using our AI model
- **Service Improvement**: To improve our diagnostic accuracy
- **Support**: To respond to your inquiries

## Data Storage & Security

- API keys are stored in iOS Keychain / Android Keystore (encrypted)
- Audio is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
- Audio recordings are processed in memory and not permanently stored

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete your local data (via Settings > Clear History)
- Request information about your data
- Contact us with privacy concerns

## Children's Privacy

Spoke is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
