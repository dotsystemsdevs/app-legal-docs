# Privacy Policy

**Mulligan: The Real Scorecard**

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mulligan ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information Collection

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data on your device:

- Round statistics including lost balls, found balls, and fore calls
- Historical round data with timestamps
- User preferences such as theme settings and haptic feedback preferences
- Custom background image references (optional, user-selected)

### 2.2 Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your device's photo library for the sole purpose of allowing you to select a custom background image. This feature is optional and requires your explicit permission.

**Important:**
- Selected images remain on your device and are not uploaded or transmitted anywhere
- The App stores only a reference (URI) to the selected image, not a copy
- Images are never shared with us or any third parties
- You can remove your custom background at any time through the Settings screen

### 2.3 Data Not Collected

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Contacts or other personal files
- The actual content of your photos (only a local reference if you choose a background)

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's secure storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to external servers. We do not have access to any information stored within the App.

Your data is protected by your device's security features, including screen lock and encryption where available.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services. No user data is shared with any external parties.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any user data with third parties. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to share.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you delete it. You may delete individual records through the App interface or remove all data by uninstalling the App. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all associated data.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any user, including children under 13. No account creation is required, and no personal data is gathered. The App is suitable for users of all ages.

---

## 8. User Rights

You maintain full control over your data:

- All data is accessible within the App
- Individual records can be deleted at any time
- Complete data removal is available by uninstalling the App

---

## 9. International Users

For users in the European Economic Area: Since we do not collect or process personal data, GDPR data processing requirements do not apply. All data remains under your control on your device.

For users in California: We do not collect or sell personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act.
