Privacy Policy

Lotty - Personal Stakes Tracker
Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Lotty respects your privacy and is designed to work without collecting personal data.

1. Data Collection

Lotty does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

All information entered in the app (such as stake values and results) is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted off the device.

2. No Servers or Third Parties

Lotty does not use any servers, cloud services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party trackers.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Since no personal data is collected or processed, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) does not apply to any data handling beyond local storage on your device.

4. Disclaimer

Lotty is an independent tracking tool for personal use. It has no affiliation with any lottery, gambling, or gaming company. The app does not provide gambling services, tips, or predictions.

5. Children's Privacy

Lotty does not knowingly collect any data from children. As the app does not collect data at all, no personal information from users of any age is stored or processed.
