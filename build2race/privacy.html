Privacy Policy

Build2Race - Triathlon Race Prep
Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Build2Race helps triathletes get ready for race day. We built it to be private by default: your data lives on your device, not on our servers. This policy explains what that means in practice.

What Stays On Your Device

Everything you create in the app is stored locally on your phone:

  • Your races, packing lists, checklists, and gear
  • Documents and photos you attach
  • Any notes or custom items you add

There is no account, no sign-in, and no Build2Race server holding your information.

What Leaves Your Device

  • Weather forecasts: to show a race-day forecast, the app sends a race's location (the town or city coordinates) to Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com), a weather service. No personal information is attached to that request.
  • Nothing else is sent anywhere.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. We do not run a push server and cannot see when, or whether, you receive them.

Photos & Files

Photos of your gear and any documents you upload are saved to the app's storage on your device. They are never uploaded to us or shared with anyone.

Analytics & Tracking

Build2Race contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, and no tracking tools. We do not build a profile of you.

Your Data, Your Control

Because everything is stored locally, you are in control. You can remove individual races, documents, and gear from within the app, and deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Build2Race is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date above always reflects the latest version.
